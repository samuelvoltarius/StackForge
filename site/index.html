ForgePix is a computational photography suite for focus stacking, astrophotography, long exposure and mosaics — in the best possible quality for further editing.

It analyses your series, drops the rejects, computes the optimal frame count and merges everything into one razor‑sharp result — and shows you why at every step.

Highlights

Everything in the box

  • One‑click Automatic — picks frames, aligns, merges, sharpens. Beginner & Pro.
  • Own engine (OpenCV/NumPy) — no external stacking software required.
  • RAW (ARW/NEF/CR2/DNG…) developed faithfully to 16‑bit — EXIF kept.
  • Camera‑Raw editor — exposure, white balance, tone curve, per‑color HSL, crop.
  • Retouch + ghost map — brush sharp areas over halos & ghosting.
  • Export presets — Instagram / WhatsApp / Web / 4K / 16‑bit print + PSD layers.
  • Batch & watch‑folder, before/after slider, film strip.
  • Fully keyboard‑operable, German & English built in.

Runs everywhere — AI is optional

Automatic works completely without AI: settings come from the measured sharpness profile. No Ollama, no server, no model download.

Optionally connect an OpenAI‑compatible server (llama.cpp / LM Studio / vLLM) or a provider key. The AI only advises & checks — it never touches your pixels.
